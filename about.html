Founded in 20xx, Lopez Lobster and Crab has always strived to provide the best quality seafood at the best possible price. However our story dates back many years before this time and is the culmination of a life time of knowledge, hardwork, and perserverance by company owner/boat captain Jose. Born in Cuba to a family of fisherman, Jose began honing his skills as an expert of the trade from a young age. Life in Cuba however was difficult with the changes brought on by the Cuban revolution and in order to provide a better life for his newborn daughter, Jose left Cuba to the U.S.

After settling in Miami, he worked a variety of different jobs always with an eye on pursuing his true passion, lobster fishing. Finally, after years of hardwork, Jose achieved his goals and founded Lopez Lobster and Crab. A staunch believer in the American dream, Jose often refers to himself as "un ingenierio de la pesca" or fishing engineer as he is always seeking new and innovative was to improve his trade.
